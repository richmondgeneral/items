# Book Condition Grading Standards

## Overview

Book grading uses a standardized scale from Fine to Poor. Accurate grading is essential for pricing and customer satisfaction.

## Grade Definitions

### Fine (F)
**As new condition**
- No defects, damage, or wear
- May have been read once with extreme care
- Dust jacket (if applicable) also Fine
- Binding tight, pages clean and bright
- **Use sparingly** - truly Fine books are rare for vintage items

### Near Fine (NF)
**Almost Fine, minimal signs of handling**
- Light shelf wear only
- Binding tight
- Pages clean, perhaps very light tanning
- Dust jacket may show minimal wear
- No writing, stamps, or labels

### Very Good (VG)
**Shows some wear but well cared for**
- Moderate shelf wear
- Binding tight or only slightly loose
- Minor foxing or tanning acceptable
- Small tears to dust jacket (under 1")
- No major defects
- **Most common grade for quality vintage books**

### Very Good- (VG-)
**Lower end of Very Good**
- Notable wear but still attractive
- Binding may be slightly loose
- More tanning/foxing
- Dust jacket may have larger tears or chips

### Good (G)
**Average used condition**
- Obvious wear throughout
- Binding may be loose
- Pages may be tanned, some foxing
- All pages and illustrations present
- May have previous owner name
- Reading copy that's still complete

### Good- (G-)
**Lower end of Good**
- Heavy wear
- Loose binding
- Significant tanning
- May have some marginalia

### Fair
**Below Good, reading copy**
- Heavy wear and soiling
- Binding loose or shaken
- May have tears or detached pages
- Previous repairs may be present
- Complete enough to read

### Poor
**Damaged but complete**
- Major defects
- Covers may be detached
- Pages may be loose
- Significant damage
- **Only acceptable for rare/valuable titles**

## Dust Jacket Grading

Dust jackets are graded separately and can significantly impact value.

| DJ Condition | Impact on Value |
|--------------|-----------------|
| Fine DJ | +50-100% or more |
| VG DJ | +25-50% |
| Good DJ | +10-25% |
| Fair/Poor DJ | Minimal addition |
| No DJ | Baseline price |

### DJ-Specific Issues
- **Chips**: Pieces missing from edges
- **Tears**: Rips in paper
- **Rubbing**: Surface wear
- **Fading**: Color loss (especially spines)
- **Price-clipped**: Corner of flap cut off (reduces value)

## Specific Defects & Terminology

### Pages
- **Foxing**: Brown spots caused by fungal growth or oxidation
- **Tanning/Browning**: Yellowing of pages from age
- **Offsetting**: Transfer of ink from facing pages
- **Marginalia**: Notes written in margins
- **Dog-eared**: Folded page corners
- **Underlining**: Text marked or highlighted

### Binding
- **Tight**: Binding secure, no looseness
- **Shaken**: Binding loose but intact
- **Cocked**: Spine slanted when viewed from above
- **Bumped**: Corners or spine ends dented
- **Rubbed**: Surface wear on cover
- **Sunned**: Faded from light exposure

### Cover/Boards
- **Soiled**: Dirty or stained
- **Ex-library**: Library stamps, pockets, labels
- **Remainder mark**: Spray paint or stamp on edges
- **Rebacked**: Spine replaced
- **Recased**: Cover re-attached to text block

## Grading Examples

### Example 1: 1930s Children's Book
- Binding tight
- Pages tanned but clean
- No dust jacket
- Small bump to corner
- **Grade: VG** (typical for age, well-preserved)

### Example 2: 1979 Dover Reprint
- Binding tight
- Pages bright
- Light cover creasing
- Price sticker residue
- **Grade: VG** (minor defects, normal use)

### Example 3: 1920s First Edition
- Binding shaken
- Foxing throughout
- Dust jacket with 2" tear and chips
- Previous owner inscription
- **Grade: Good (book) / Fair (DJ)**

## Pricing Adjustments by Grade

Using VG as baseline (100%):

| Grade | Typical % of VG Price |
|-------|----------------------|
| Fine | 200-300%+ |
| NF | 125-175% |
| VG | 100% (baseline) |
| VG- | 75-90% |
| Good | 50-75% |
| Good- | 35-50% |
| Fair | 20-35% |
| Poor | 10-20% |

## Photography for Condition

When photographing for listings, capture:

1. **Cover** - Overall condition visible
2. **Spine** - Show any fading, cocking, wear
3. **Corners** - Bumps, wear
4. **Pages** - Tanning, foxing, cleanliness
5. **Any defects** - Close-up of specific issues
6. **Copyright page** - Edition identification
7. **Dust jacket** - All flaps, back, spine
