The Story

A vintage gem from the Space Race era! This 1967 Step-Up Book captures the magic of aviation history—from the Wright Brothers' first flight at Kitty Hawk to the Mercury astronauts reaching for the stars.

Written by Mary Lee Settle (who would later win the National Book Award) and illustrated by George Evans, a legendary EC Comics artist known for his aviation work. Evans brought the same meticulous attention to detail that made him famous in titles like "Aces High."

These Step-Up Books were Random House's answer to young readers hungry for real knowledge—substantive content delivered in an accessible format. This particular title arrived just a year before Apollo 8 orbited the moon, making it a perfect time capsule of Space Age optimism.
