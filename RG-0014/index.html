The Story

Richmond General • Est. 2024

This promotional LP was recorded live at Wrigley Field in July 1969, capturing the electric atmosphere of what Cubs fans believed would finally be their championship year. The album features player interviews and the beloved Bleacher Bums fan group.

The cover showcases the legendary roster including four future Hall of Famers: Ernie Banks, Ron Santo, Billy Williams, and Ferguson Jenkins. This was the team that led the NL East most of the season before their historic September collapse to the Miracle Mets.
