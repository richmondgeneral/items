History & Provenance

Harold Gray's beloved Depression-era comic strip captured America's heart with the irrepressible optimism of little Annie and her loyal dog Sandy. This 1979 Dover reprint preserves the complete daily strips from 1931—the year Annie first met the enigmatic Daddy Warbucks and embarked on adventures that would make her a cultural icon.

Dover's faithful reproduction maintains the original artistry and storytelling that made "Little Orphan Annie" one of the most influential comic strips of the 20th century, inspiring the Broadway musical and generations of readers.
