History & Craftsmanship

This classic American pressed-back bar stool showcases the fine craftsmanship of the early 1900s. The ornate scrollwork on the backrest was created using a steam-press technique popular during the Victorian and Edwardian eras. Turned spindle supports and a swivel seat made these stools favorites in ice cream parlors, soda fountains, and home bars across America.
